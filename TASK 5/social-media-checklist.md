Stay Safe Online Checklist for Indian Students : 

1- Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication): Activate 2FA on all social media (Instagram, WhatsApp) and university email accounts to prevent unauthorized access.
2- Secure UPI Transactions (Financial): Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. Only enter the PIN when sending money. If someone asks for a PIN to receive money, it is a scam.
3- Avoid Suspicious Links (Phishing): Never click on links in unsolicited SMS, WhatsApp messages, or emails promising cash prizes, job offers, or urgent KYC updates.
4- Use Unique Passwords & Managers: Use a unique, complex password for each account (e.g., banking vs. social media) and store them in a secure password manager rather than browser memory.
5- Disable Public Wi-Fi for Banking: Avoid using café, airport, or hostel public Wi-Fi for banking apps or UPI transactions. Use a VPN if you must access sensitive data.
6- Update Apps & OS Regularly: Regularly update your phone and laptop operating systems and apps, especially payment apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), to patch security vulnerabilities.
7- Secure UPI/Banking Apps (Financial): Set a separate screen lock/app lock for your UPI and mobile banking apps, distinct from your phone unlock PIN.
8- Check Privacy Settings: Review social media privacy settings frequently to restrict who can see your phone number, email address, and personal information
