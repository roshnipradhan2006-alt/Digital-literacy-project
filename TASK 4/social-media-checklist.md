Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

Do’s: 
1- Use your platforms to showcase your skills, projects, internships, and professional passion.
2- Be professional and respectful when creating your social media profile.
3- Ensure you use strong passwords to protect your social media account from being hacked.
4- Only post positive information or share something good, but always verify information before posting it on social media.
5- Review who can see your content on each platform to protect your privacy, especially as policies change.

Don’ts: 
1- Don’t post your personal information such as your phone number or password on social media.
2- Don’t post offensive or disrespectful things about others on social media.
3- Don’t click on any suspicious links or messages from people you don’t know.
4- Don’t post false information or rumors on social media.
5- Remember that "private" content can still be screenshotted and shared by others instantly.
