Digital-literacy 

# Project Overview:
This repository holds my Digital Literacy project that I completed as part of the CSE0001 course in VIT Bhopal University. The aim of this project was to assist the student in understanding the ways in which digital tools could be utilized effectively and in the right manner in the academic and professional fields. In the present world, it is not just enough to know how to use technology, but it is also important to understand safe internet usage, professional online communication, and having a good digital presence.

With the help of this project, I was able to complete five specific tasks that concentrated on various aspects of digital literacy, such as digital content creation, professional portfolio development, collaboration tools, email etiquette, and cybercrime awareness. With the aid of these specific tasks, I was able to gain hands-on knowledge and experience of the actual platforms and tools that are utilized by the student in their academic life. 

# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
For this task, I designed a one-page digital literacy awareness infographic using an online design platform such as Canva. The aim of the infographic was to provide an explanation of what digital literacy is and the importance of digital literacy for students in the current technology-driven world. The ideas presented in the infographic include the importance of safe internet use, the importance of digital tools for students, and the importance of having a professional online presence.

From the design of the infographic, I learned that visual communication is essential in the sense that it makes it easier to understand and present ideas. In the case of the infographic, it is not necessary to include text to present the ideas, as the graphics and icons are enough to present the ideas and grab the attention of the readers. This task helped me understand the importance of digital literacy for students in the current technology-driven world.

Folder: task-1-presentation


# Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
For this task, I created and updated my profiles on professional sites such as GitHub and LinkedIn. These sites are very popular among students and working professionals to showcase their work, skills, and academic accomplishments.

Creating these profiles has given me the opportunity to learn the significance of creating a professional digital identity from the very beginning of my academic life. A digital portfolio provides an opportunity for students to showcase their work and share their learning process with other professionals or recruiters. This task has motivated me to keep updating my digital identity during the course of my engineering studies.

Folder: task-2-portfolio


# Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms
In this task, I was required to look at coding practice platforms and online collaboration platforms. I was able to sign up on a coding practice website and solve a beginner-level code to get an idea of how coding practice websites operate. Such websites assist students in developing their logical reasoning and coding skills through practice problems.

In addition to this, I also developed a Google form quiz on digital literacy awareness. This form contains different types of questions and may be used to test students on their digital literacy awareness. This task assisted me in learning how digital platforms may be used to facilitate learning and collaboration among students.

Folder: task-3-platforms


# Task 4 – Email Etiquette and Digital Communication
This task was about digital communication. In the academic or professional world, email is considered one of the most common means of communication. Therefore, it is vital to know how to communicate effectively through email.

In this task, I had to write two different professional emails. One email was to ask the professor if I could extend the deadline for an assignment. The second email was to ask an internship coordinator about an opportunity for an internship during the summer. These two emails followed the etiquette for writing an email. The subject, greeting, email message, and closing were all correctly included.

I had to create a checklist about the dos and don’ts of using social media. This task helped me understand the importance of communication style.

Folder: task-4-email-etiquette

# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
The last task required me to focus on cybercrime awareness. In this task, I had to conduct research on a cybercrime scenario and prepare a case study where I had to explain the ways through which cybercrime occurs, who are the victims of cybercrime, and what are the consequences of cybercrime. In addition, I had to prepare a checklist for the prevention of cybercrime, which can be followed by the students. The tips for the prevention of cybercrime include the protection of personal information, being cautious about suspicious links, and being cautious during online payment transactions.

This task helped me understand the importance of cybercrime awareness.

Folder: task-5-cybercrime
